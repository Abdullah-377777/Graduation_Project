Our Breakthrough Project

Harnessing artificial intelligence to save lives through early cancer detection

Advanced Dataset Analysis

Our project utilizes the GSE19804 dataset with 114 samples and 54,676 gene features, enabling our machine learning models to identify patterns distinguishing cancerous from healthy lung tissue with high precision.

Sophisticated ML Pipeline

Through data cleaning, normalization, and hybrid feature selection (ANOVA F-value, Boruta, Markov Blanket), we’ve developed a robust system to identify critical gene biomarkers for classification.

Comprehensive Evaluation

Our models are evaluated using accuracy, precision, recall, F1 score, and AUC, ensuring reliable predictions to support clinical decision-making.
